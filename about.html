📖 About CodeCanvas

CodeCanvas is a project-based learning platform designed for absolute beginners who want to master HTML and CSS by building real, meaningful projects — not just theory.

Each project increases in complexity, giving you a clear roadmap from your first line of code to creating responsive, modern websites. We believe coding is a superpower, and our mission is to make web development accessible, fun, and practical for everyone.

All projects include learning objectives, skills breakdown, student tasks, challenges, and pro tips. You'll also find a preview placeholder to imagine the final result. Happy coding!

CodeCanvas — Build with purpose, learn by doing.
