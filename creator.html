Why this website was created?
I built CodeCanvas because I remember the struggle of finding project-based, beginner-friendly resources. Theory is not enough — building real things is what makes skills stick.

Mission: To help every new developer gain confidence through practical projects. I break down complex concepts into digestible steps so anyone can learn HTML/CSS without feeling overwhelmed.

💬 Encouragement: Consistency beats intensity. Code for 20 minutes every day, break things, fix them, and celebrate small wins. You belong here — let’s grow together!
